SAAS SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT
Effective Date: January 15, 2025
Between: Acme Corp ("Provider") and GlobalTech Inc ("Customer")

1. SERVICES. Provider grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable license
to access the CloudSync Platform for the Subscription Term. Service availability
target is 99.9% monthly uptime, measured excluding scheduled maintenance windows
(Sundays 02:00-06:00 UTC).

2. FEES. Customer shall pay $12,500/month, billed annually in advance ($150,000).
Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month. Provider may suspend access after
30 days of non-payment. Fees increase by up to 7% upon renewal unless negotiated.

3. DATA. Customer retains all ownership of Customer Data. Provider may process
Customer Data solely to deliver the Services. Upon termination, Provider shall
export Customer Data in CSV format within 30 days, then delete all copies within
90 days. Provider maintains SOC 2 Type II certification.

4. TERM. Initial term is 24 months. Auto-renews for successive 12-month periods
unless either party provides 90 days written notice of non-renewal. Early
termination by Customer requires payment of 50% of remaining fees.

5. LIABILITY. Provider's total aggregate liability shall not exceed fees paid in
the 12 months preceding the claim. Neither party is liable for indirect,
consequential, or punitive damages. This limitation does not apply to breaches
of confidentiality or data protection obligations.

6. INDEMNIFICATION. Provider indemnifies Customer against third-party IP
infringement claims arising from use of the Platform as permitted herein.
Customer indemnifies Provider against claims arising from Customer Data or
Customer's violation of applicable law.
