CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT
Effective Date: March 1, 2025
Between: Acme Corp ("Consultant") and GlobalTech Inc ("Client")

1. SERVICES. Consultant shall provide cloud migration advisory services as
described in each Statement of Work ("SOW"). Initial SOW covers assessment and
planning for migration of Client's on-premise ERP system to AWS, estimated at
480 consultant-hours over 12 weeks.

2. FEES. Consultant charges $275/hour for Senior Consultants and $175/hour for
Associates. Invoiced monthly in arrears, net-30 payment terms. Travel expenses
reimbursed at cost with prior approval for amounts exceeding $500. Total
engagement cap for the initial SOW is $180,000 unless Client approves overruns
in writing.

3. DELIVERABLES. Key deliverables: (a) Current State Assessment (Week 4),
(b) Migration Architecture Document (Week 8), (c) Risk Assessment and
Mitigation Plan (Week 10), (d) Final Migration Roadmap (Week 12). Client has
10 business days to review each deliverable. Acceptance is deemed granted if
no written objection is received within the review period.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY. All pre-existing IP remains with its owner. Work
product created specifically for Client under this Agreement ("Deliverables")
is owned by Client upon full payment. Consultant retains a license to use
general methodologies, frameworks, and know-how developed during the engagement.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY. Each party shall protect Confidential Information with the
same degree of care as its own, but no less than reasonable care, for 3 years
after disclosure. Confidential Information excludes information that is publicly
available, independently developed, or rightfully received from a third party.

6. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. Client
pays for all work completed and expenses incurred through the termination date.
Consultant shall deliver all work-in-progress materials within 10 business days
of termination. Non-solicitation of each other's employees applies for 12 months.

7. LIABILITY. Consultant's liability is capped at the fees paid under the
applicable SOW. Consultant carries professional liability insurance of at least
$2,000,000 per occurrence.
