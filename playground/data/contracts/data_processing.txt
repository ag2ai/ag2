DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT
Effective Date: January 15, 2025
Between: Acme Corp ("Processor") and GlobalTech Inc ("Controller")

1. SCOPE. This DPA applies to all Personal Data processed by Processor on behalf
of Controller under the SaaS Subscription Agreement dated January 15, 2025.
Processing activities include storage, indexing, analytics, and backup of
customer records containing names, email addresses, and usage metadata.

2. OBLIGATIONS OF PROCESSOR. Processor shall: (a) process Personal Data only on
documented instructions from Controller; (b) ensure personnel are bound by
confidentiality; (c) implement appropriate technical and organizational security
measures including AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit;
(d) assist Controller with data subject access requests within 10 business days;
(e) delete or return all Personal Data upon termination per the main Agreement.

3. SUB-PROCESSORS. Processor currently uses: AWS (us-east-1, eu-west-1) for
infrastructure, Datadog for monitoring, and Snowflake for analytics. Processor
shall notify Controller 30 days before engaging new sub-processors. Controller
may object within 14 days; unresolved objections entitle Controller to terminate
the affected service without penalty.

4. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS. Transfers outside the EEA rely on Standard
Contractual Clauses (EU 2021/914, Module 2). Processor shall conduct Transfer
Impact Assessments annually and notify Controller of government access requests
unless legally prohibited.

5. BREACH NOTIFICATION. Processor shall notify Controller of any Personal Data
breach without undue delay and no later than 48 hours after becoming aware.
Notification shall include nature of breach, categories of data affected,
approximate number of records, and remediation measures taken.

6. AUDIT RIGHTS. Controller may audit Processor's compliance once per year with
30 days notice. Processor shall make available all information necessary to
demonstrate compliance. Controller bears audit costs unless the audit reveals
material non-compliance.
